Session Info

Sessions will take place entirely outdoors in all weathers (unless high winds or thunder is forecast). Both children and adults attending will need to dress appropriately for the conditions. Please consider:

  • ❧ waterproof trousers (the site can get very muddy!)
  • ❧ lots of layers in winter
  • ❧ warm gloves, hats, scarves and socks in winter
  • ❧ long sleeves/trousers all year round
  • ❧ hats and suncream in summer
  • ❧ sturdy footwear which covers the whole foot
